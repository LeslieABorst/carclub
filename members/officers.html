January 5, 2018

To all Plymouth Owner's Club Region Mmbers:

The outgoing board of directors had voted to place the following candidates into office prior to receiving all of the member's voteing ballots in order to keep the club running efficiently. In the event that a write-in candidate prevails, that particular officer will be replaced by the write-in candidate.Ballots are going to be inserted into the upcoming Plymouth Bulletin #347 and ALL ballots will be due no later than April 30, 2018.

Your new officers are as follows:

  • Russ Nardi becomes President
  • Jeff Berkheimer becomes Vice President
  • John Anderson becomes Correspinding Secretary
  • Jim Benjaminson remains in his current positions

Additonally:

  • Lanny Knutson continues his postion as Editor
  • Larry Neusch moves over to begin his term as a new Director 2018-2023
  • Nick DeSimone remains as Director until 2019
  • Joe Suminski remains as Judge Director
  • Carl Wegner's term is up but he is willing to remain as the third Director on an interim basis.

Any questions or comments, feel free to contact me directly.

Regards,
